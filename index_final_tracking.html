Common questions about HisaabNow

Everything you'd ask a friend who already uses it.

What exactly is HisaabNow?
HisaabNow is a POS billing app built for Indian small businesses — cafés, salons, restaurants, sweet shops, and kirana stores. It does billing, GST invoicing, inventory, and customer udhaar in one app, but the thing that sets it apart is real profit tracking on every sale. Most billing apps tell you what you sold. HisaabNow tells you what you actually earned, after costs.
Which businesses can use HisaabNow?
It's designed for five business types: cafés & coffee shops (recipe cost tracking, voice billing for the morning rush), salons & barber shops (stylist commissions, repeat-customer alerts), restaurants (table-wise billing, kitchen ticket printing, per-dish profit), sweet shops & mithai stores (weight-based pricing, festival inventory, expiry tracking), and kirana & grocery stores (barcode scanning, udhaar khaata with WhatsApp reminders). One app, five vertical-tuned experiences.
How is this different from Khatabook, Vyapar, or myBillBook?
Most Indian billing software tracks revenue. We track real profit. When you sell a cappuccino, HisaabNow auto-deducts the actual cost (beans, milk, cup) and shows you what you earned — not just what the customer paid. Same for stylist services, restaurant dishes, sweet shop kg-pricing. Plus voice billing in Hindi, an AI Munim that flags problems before they kill your month, and pricing that starts at ₹10 for 7 days. We're built for shopkeepers who care about margins, not just bills printed.
Does HisaabNow work in Hindi?
Yes. Full Hindi UI plus Hindi voice billing — say "do cappuccino, ek brownie" and the app adds them to the bill. Voice recognition handles Hindi numerals (ek, do, teen, char...) so you can bill hands-free during the rush. The whole interface, every screen, every button, switches between English and Hindi with one tap.
Does it handle GST invoicing?
Yes. HisaabNow generates GST-compliant bills with HSN codes, GSTIN, CGST/SGST/IGST splits, and proper invoice formatting. You can set GST rates per item, mark items as exempt, and export reports for filing. WhatsApp-ready receipts go to customers in two taps.
Can I track stylist commissions, dish profit, or customer udhaar?
Yes — these are core features for the verticals that need them. Salons get per-stylist daily totals, services, tips, commission. Restaurants get per-dish profit (Munim flags any dish priced below cost). Kirana stores get a udhaar khaata with auto-WhatsApp reminders to customers whose debts cross 7 or 14 days. Sweet shops get expiry alerts to discount before discarding. Cafés get ingredient-stock alerts when a key ingredient is running out.
Do I need a separate barcode scanner or printer?
No. Barcode scanning works with your phone camera — point, beep, item added. If you want a USB scanner for speed at busy counters, the app supports those too. For receipts: WhatsApp share, PDF download, or any standard Bluetooth thermal printer. Restaurants can connect a kitchen-side printer for ticket printing.

No setup fee, no per-bill charge, payment via Google Play. The first 15 bills on trial are free before any charge.
