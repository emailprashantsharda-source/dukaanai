Common questions about Grocery Stores POS

Real questions from grocery stores owners using HisaabNow.

How does udhaar khaata with WhatsApp reminders work?
Add customer's name and phone number once. Mark a bill as udhaar (credit) — it goes to their khaata. After 7 days unpaid, the app sends a polite WhatsApp reminder. After 14 days, a stronger nudge. After 21 days, it alerts you to call directly. You don't lift a finger. Built specifically for the way Indian kirana store billing software needs to work.
Can I scan barcodes with my phone camera?
Yes. Open the bill screen, tap scan, point camera at barcode — beep, item added, total updated. Works with most product barcodes (atta packs, oil bottles, soap, biscuits). For items without printed barcodes (loose grain), search by name or use a custom code. USB barcode scanners also supported for high-volume counters.
How does it handle MRP-based pricing?
Most kirana items have a printed MRP. Set MRP and your purchase cost — the app calculates margin automatically. You can also set a discount (₹2 off MRP, common for regulars) and the app tracks how much you give in discounts vs your real margin. Useful for spotting items where you're over-discounting.
What about loose grain or flour sold by weight?
Set price per kg for loose items (atta ₹35/kg, dal ₹110/kg). At the counter, type the weight and the bill calculates automatically. Works the same way as sweet shop weight pricing — common for Indian kirana stores selling everything from rice to spices loose.
Does it support multiple shops or just one?
Multi-shop is included on the annual plan. Run two or more kirana stores with separate inventory, separate udhaar khaata, separate staff logins per shop — but consolidated profit reports across all shops. Common for owners with one main store and one neighborhood branch.
How does it compare to Khatabook for udhaar tracking?
Khatabook does udhaar well — that's its core feature. HisaabNow does udhaar plus billing, GST, barcode, stock, profit, and customer management in one app. If you only need a digital khaata, Khatabook works. If you want to actually run the shop end-to-end with one app, HisaabNow. Many of our users were on Khatabook before and switched to consolidate.
