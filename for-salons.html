Common questions about Salons POS

Real questions from salons owners using HisaabNow.

How does HisaabNow track stylist commissions?
Each stylist gets their own profile with daily totals: services done, total billed, tips received, commission earned. You set commission rate per stylist (flat % or tiered by service type). At week-end, the app shows you exactly what to pay each stylist — no arguments, no spreadsheets. Built specifically for Indian salon billing software needs.
Can I manage walk-ins and appointments together?
Yes. Walk-ins go straight to billing — pick services, assign stylist, done. For appointments, you can schedule slots in advance, and when the client arrives, the appointment converts to an active bill in one tap. Both flow into the same daily report.
What about retail product sales (shampoos, hair oils) alongside services?
Add retail products as separate items in your catalog with their own MRP and cost. Bill them on the same receipt as services — the app tracks service revenue and product revenue separately so you know which side of your business is actually earning.
Can each stylist log in to see their own daily commission?
Yes. Each stylist gets their own login. They see their day — services completed, tips, total commission earned — but not the salon's overall numbers. Owner sees everything. This is unique to HisaabNow among Indian POS apps for salons.
How does it handle package deals and pre-paid memberships?
Create a package (e.g., "6 haircuts for ₹2,000") with the customer's phone number. App tracks remaining sessions automatically — when they walk in, you see how many haircuts they have left. No paper cards to lose, no arguments about what's been used.
Does it work for single-chair barber shops too?
Yes — and it's designed for them. Many of our salon and barber shop POS users are single-chair operators. Skip the multi-stylist setup, run lean, just bill and track profit. Same ₹10 trial, same ₹490 annual, scaled to your size.
